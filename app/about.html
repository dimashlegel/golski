ABOUT COMPANY

The confectionery factory "GOLSKI SWEET COMPANY" began its work in 2016 and specializes in production of wafer and candy products under its own brand «GOLSKI». The factory is located in the ecologically clean region of Ukraine – Chortkiv town in Ternopil region. Factory production lines are certified with HACCP, ISO 22000. The factory sells its products both in Ukraine and foreign markets. Currently GOLSKI's products are sold in such markets as Poland, Germany, France, Romania, Moldova, Lithuania, Belarus, Iraq, the countries of the Caucasus and Central Asia.
